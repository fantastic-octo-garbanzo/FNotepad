1. Which file formats can I open?
With FNotepad you can open all file formats, but not all file formats are displayed legibly.

2. Is FNotepad free?
Yes, FNotepad is free.

3. How do I open files?
Click File, then click Open, or press Ctrl + O.

3. How do I save files?
Click File, then click Save or Save As ..., or press Ctrl + S on the keyboard.

4. How do I create a new file?
Click File, then click New File, or press Ctrl + N.

5. How do I create a new window?
Click File, then click New Window, or press Ctrl + G.

6. How do I undo an unwanted change?
Click Edit, then click Undo, or press Ctrl + Z.

7. How do I cut something?
Click Edit, then click Cut, or press Ctrl + X.

8. How do I add something?
Click Edit, then click Paste, or press Ctrl + V.

9. How do I copy something?
Click Edit, then click Copy, or press Ctrl + C.

10. How do I delete something?
Click Edit, then click Paste, or press the Delete key.

11. How do I search for something?
Click Edit, then Find ..., or press Ctrl + F.

12. How do I select the complete text?
Click Edit, then click Select All, or press Ctrl + A.

13. How do I insert the date and time?
Click Edit, then click Time / Date, or press the F5 key.

14. How do I open the terminal?
Click Edit, then click Open Terminal, or press Ctrl + T.

15. How do I change the font?
Click Format, then click Font ...

16. How do I activate the line break?
Click Format, and then click Word Wrap.

17. How do I change the text color?
Click Format and then Text Color ...

18. How do I change the background color?
Click Format and then Background Color ...

19. How do I switch to English?
Click Language and then Deutsch.

20. How do I switch to Italian?
Click Language and than Italiano.
