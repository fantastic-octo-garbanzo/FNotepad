Help

=====

1. What file formats can I open?

With FNotepad you can basically open all file formats, but not all file formats are displayed readably.

2. Is FNotepad free of charge?

Yes, FNotepad is free of charge.

3. How do I open files?

Click File and then Open, or press the key combination Ctrl + O.

3. How do I save files?

Click File and then Save or Save As..., or press the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + S.

4. How do I create a new file?

Click File and then New File, or press the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + N.

5. How do I create a new window?

Click File and then New Window, or press the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + G.

6. How do I undo an unwanted change?

Click Edit and then Undo, or press the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + Z.

7. How do I cut something out?

Click Edit and then Cut, or press the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + X.

8. How do I paste something?

Click Edit and then Paste, or press the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + V.

9. How do I copy something?

Click Edit and then Copy, or press the keyboard shortcut Ctrl + C.

10. How do I delete something?

Click Edit and then Paste, or press the Delete key.

11. How do I search for something?

Click on Edit and then on Find..., or press the key combination Ctrl + F.

12. How do I select all the text?

Click on Edit and then on Select All, or press the key combination Ctrl + A.

13. How do I insert the date and time?

Click on Edit and then on Time/Date, or press the F5 key.

14. How do I open the terminal?

Click Edit and then Open Terminal, or press the Ctrl + T key combination.

15. How do I change the font?

Click on Format and then on Font....

16. How do I activate the line break?

Click on Format and then on Word Wrap.

17. How do I change the text color?

Click on Format and then on Text Color....

18. How do I change the background color?

Click on Format and then on Background Color....

19. How do I change to English?

Click on Language and click on English.

20. How do I change to Italian?

Click on Language and on Italiano.
